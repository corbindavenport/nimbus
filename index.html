Nimbus Privacy Policy

Weather data is retrieved from Yahoo Weather, which is subject to the Yahoo Weather Privacy Policy. Note that the only data sent online is your ZIP Code, which is used to retrieve weather data. The weather radar is retrieved from Freese-Notis Weather, which is subject to their Privacy Policy.

Nimbus uses Google Analytics by default, which is a web analysis service provided by Google. This is used by the developer(s) of Nimbus to determine how users interact with Nimbus, and help develop the app to better suit it's users. Google utilizes the data collected to track and examine the use of Nimbus, to prepare reports on its activities and share them with other Google services. Google may use the data collected to contextualize and personalize the ads of its own advertising network. The use of Google Analytics is subject to Google's Privacy Policy, which can be found here. However, all Google Analytics services can be completely disabled by the user in the Nimbus app settings. Furthermore, none of your app settings (ZIP code, unit of measurement, theme, etc) are sent through Analytics.

Nibmus includes functionality to share the current weather forecast (or radar image) for the specified location through Twitter and Tumblr. By sharing this information through Twitter and Tumblr, you are subject to the privacy policies of Twitter and/or Tumblr. The current temperature, current weather conditions, and the currently specified location are passed to those services only when one of the share buttons is selected. If the radar image is shared, only the URL to the generated image is sent to those services.
